Solve for n.
\begin{align*}
    \sqrt{E[(\hat{F}_n(x) - F(x))^2]} &\leq \frac{1}{400} \\
    \sqrt{\frac{1}{4n}} &= \frac{1}{400} \tag*{Since $max\{E(\hat{F}_n(x) - F(x))^2\} = \frac{1}{4n}$} \\
    \frac{1}{4n} &= \frac{1}{160000} \\
    \frac{1}{n} &= \frac{1}{40000} \\
    n &= 40000
\end{align*}
